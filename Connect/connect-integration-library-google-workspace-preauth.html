About
Retell requests read-only access to Drive, Sheets, and Docs. You'll approve the specific scopes in Google's consent screen. The connection is scoped to this workspace.
After signing in, add Google Drive as a source in the Knowledge Base section.

Google Workspace connects your Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Google Calendar to Retell.

Knowledge Base: Add Google Drive as a source in the Knowledge Base section. Selected folders sync automatically so agents can retrieve files mid-call via semantic search.

Calendar tools: Once connected, agents can check availability, create, update, and cancel events on Google Calendar directly from a call.

Drive tools: Agents can fetch a specific file by name or search across your Drive scope mid-call — useful for retrieving contracts, SOPs, or any document a caller references.
