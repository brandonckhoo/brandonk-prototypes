Callers keep getting talked over when they pause to think. Why is that happening?
Direct answer

The agent jumps in the instant it thinks the caller stopped. In a flow, that's an agent-wide Speech setting: Response Eagerness at its max of 1, with Dynamically adjust off.

It's agent-wide, so it applies to every node — including Welcome and Book Appointment.

What I'd change

Ease off the eagerness so the agent waits out a pause, and let it adapt to the caller's pace.
