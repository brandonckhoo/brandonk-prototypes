Flowchart illustrating how cardholder data flows through Retell AI's isolated Secure Payment Module directly to Stripe,
bypassing the Voice Engine and LLM Agent. Sensitive payment data never enters the conversational AI layer.

Flowchart (top to bottom)

1
Voice conversation begins. The customer calls in and speaks with the Retell AI voice agent normally. Speech is transcribed by the Voice Engine and processed by the LLM Agent.
2
Secure payment mode activates. When payment is needed, the agent enters a secure isolation mode. The LLM and Voice Engine are walled off from the payment data path.
3
Cardholder data is captured in isolation. The customer enters their card details using their phone keypad (DTMF). The tones are routed directly to the Secure Payment Module, bypassing both the Voice Engine and LLM Agent entirely. Because cardholder data never touches the conversational AI layer, this architecture meets PCI DSS Level 1 certification requirements for secure voice payment processing.
4
Stripe processes the payment. The Secure Payment Module sends tokenized card data to Stripe's API. Stripe processes the charge and returns the result.
5
Secure mode exits. The isolated payment session ends. No card data was ever stored, logged, or exposed to the conversational AI layer.
6
Customer hears the result. The voice agent confirms whether the payment was successful or failed, and the normal conversation resumes.
