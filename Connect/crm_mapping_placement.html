Why Option B scales better over time

Field mappings
Agent
Tool config
Each feature owns its own config. Integrations stays clean.
The analogy. Think of Integrations like the power outlet on your wall. You plug something in (auth) but you don't configure the lamp, the TV, and the toaster all at the outlet. Each appliance has its own controls. CRM sync mappings are the appliance's controls, not the outlet's.
