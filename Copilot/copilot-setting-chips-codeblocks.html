Callers keep getting talked over when they pause to think. Why is that happening?
Direct answer

The agent jumps in the instant it thinks the caller stopped. That's Response Eagerness at its max of 1, so any short pause reads as "caller finished." Dynamically adjust is also off, so it never eases up mid-thought.

What I'd change

Ease off the eagerness so the agent waits out a pause, and let it adapt to the caller's pace.
