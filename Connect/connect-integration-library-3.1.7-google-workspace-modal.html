Agent tools
Functions your agents can call during conversations. Toggle off any you don't want exposed.
search_knowledge_base(query)
Semantic search across all synced Google Drive, Sheets, and Docs content. Returns top-matching passages.
get_document(doc_id)
Fetch full content of a specific Drive or Docs file by ID. Use when a passage needs full context.
lookup_sheet_row(sheet, filters)
Query a synced Google Sheet for a specific row — e.g., price by SKU, availability by region.
create_calendar_event(title, time, attendees)
Book a meeting on the authorized user's Google Calendar. Sends invites to attendees.
send_gmail(to, subject, body)
Send an email from the authorized Gmail account — e.g., a follow-up summary after the call.
Description
Connect Retell to Google Workspace to sync knowledge from Drive, Sheets, and Docs into your agents' knowledge base, and to let agents read your Gmail, send email, and create calendar events during or after calls.
