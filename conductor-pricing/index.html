Conductor

Credits & Overage

Credit based freemium. 30 free credits per user per day, 1 credit per message. Past the free limit, if the workspace has overage on, Conductor keeps working and bills $0.20 per credit up to the monthly cap. If overage is off, Conductor pauses until the daily reset. There is no buy-credits purchase.
The flow
1
Open and build. User clicks into Conductor and starts chatting. Each message spends 1 credit from their 30 free daily credits.
2
Track usage. The credit pill in the header counts down. Opening it shows credits remaining, when they reset at midnight, and the workspace overage status.
3
Low warning. With a few credits left, a heads-up appears with two paths, keep going or wait for the free reset tomorrow.
4
Hit zero. Overage on means Conductor keeps working and bills $0.20 per message, no interruption. Overage off means Conductor pauses until reset, and an admin can enable overage to continue.
5
Pay path. Enabling overage (org level, admin only, with a monthly cap) is the entire keep-going-and-pay mechanism. No buy-credits or top-up screen.
