Flowchart illustrating how Retell AI agents receive calls routed from Five9 VCC, respect outbound compliance rules
(TCPA, DNC, state hours), and transfer to human agents with full conversation context for unified contact center operations.
How it works
0
Compliance rules apply before dial. Five9 enforces outbound compliance rules (TCPA time windows, DNC lists, state level restrictions like no calls in Georgia on Sunday at 8am, maximum retry rules) before any call leaves the platform. This is the primary reason Caribou and other outbound customers rely on Five9.
1
Call originates. Either a customer calls in (inbound) or Five9's dialer places an outbound call. The call flows through Five9 VCC first for routing, compliance checks, and campaign assignment.
2
Five9 routes to Retell over SIP. Five9 VCC sends the call to Retell's SIP endpoint based on routing rules configured in Five9 admin. Call metadata (caller ID, campaign name, skill, queue, IVR selections) flows alongside the audio stream.
3
Retell agent handles the call. The Retell AI agent picks up with full context from Five9. Agent behavior is configured in Retell (prompt, tools, integrations) while call routing and compliance stay in Five9.
4
Voice conversation happens. Agent converses with the caller naturally, using connected Retell integrations (CRM lookup, knowledge base, scheduling) as needed during the call.
5
Caller requests a human. When the conversation needs a human (complex issue, policy exception, caller preference), the AI agent initiates a warm transfer.
6
Warm transfer with context. Retell hands the call back to Five9 with conversation summary, caller intent, and collected data attached. The human agent in Five9 receives the call already briefed, not starting from scratch.
7
Five9 routes to skilled agent. Five9 applies its normal skill and queue routing to find the right human agent. The AI-to-human handoff looks identical to a caller transfer between two humans.
8
Call outcome logs. When the call ends, Retell writes the outcome, duration, summary, and recording link back to Five9 as a call activity.
9
Unified Five9 reporting. AI and human calls appear in the same Five9 reporting views so ops teams track volume, handle time, and outcomes across both. No separate Retell dashboard required for core contact center metrics.
