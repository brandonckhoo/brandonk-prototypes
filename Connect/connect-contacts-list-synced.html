What this is. A unified contact directory that merges people from every CRM and helpdesk you've connected — Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, and anyone you've added manually — into one row per person, deduped by phone and email.

Why we sync. At call start, the agent has ~300ms to know who's calling. Hitting three separate CRM APIs live is too slow and fragile — so we mirror your contacts into Retell and refresh in the background. One lookup, one source of truth, no cold starts on the first "Hi {{name}}."

What the agent gets. Name, tier, open tickets, lifecycle stage, owner — whatever you mapped during integration setup, available as {{contact.*}} variables in every prompt and conv-flow node.

What happens after a call. Conversation memory (the bullets below on the right) gets written back against the contact — so the next agent on the next call picks up where the last one left off, across channels. Writebacks to the source CRM (logged call, updated field, new ticket) are configured per-agent in the Conv Flow.

Sync cadence. Incremental every 5 min via webhooks where the source supports it (HubSpot, Zendesk), 15-min poll otherwise (SFDC custom objects). Full reconciliation nightly.
