Overview

Retell AI builds production-grade voice agents that answer calls, qualify leads, book appointments, and resolve customer issues in natural conversation. This integration connects Retell to your Google Workspace so agents can ground their answers in your real business content.

Attach Google Drive folders, Sheets, and Docs as knowledge sources that sync into Retell's Knowledge Base every 30 minutes. Agents retrieve the right content via semantic search during the call, so they answer from the source of truth instead of hallucinating. Drive is also available as mid-call Functions (Get File, Search Drive) so agents can fetch a specific file when the caller asks for it.

Retell requests read-only access. You decide which folders and files are in scope, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google Account.

What you can do
  • Sync Drive folders as a knowledge source for semantic retrieval during calls
  • Read specific Sheet rows mid-call by key (e.g. order status, pricing lookup)
  • Fetch a Doc by URL or ID as a Conversational Flow node
  • Incremental sync every 30 minutes with full audit logs in the Data tab
  • Scoped per workspace, revokable from your Google Account at any time
